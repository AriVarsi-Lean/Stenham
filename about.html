About Our School

Stenham Private School is a fully registered independent South African private primary school, that prescribes and implements a curriculum for physical, mental, emotional, intellectual and spiritual progress for achieving multi-faceted development and growth of the younger generation. The School provides an environment for the development of quality leadership, team spirit, sportsmanship, self-esteem and other social attributes to prepare children to be future leaders and ensure continuity and growth for the South African nation. The School provides a happy, caring and safe environment for your child. We work hard to make sure that our learners have a positive experience at Stenham Private School, which encourages a love of learning and self-discipline from the very start.

Awesome Education

The curriculum of the School is well planned with the priority given to developing high standards of literacy and numeracy. The Stenham Private School work is supported by a good level of resources and additional staffing to meet the individual needs of the children. We go the extra mile to ensure each child has acquired the study skills necessary for them to continue their development at the next stage of education.

Awesome classes

The School facilities are all located on the ground floor to accommodate learners with special needs. All facilities including the classrooms, administration offices, management offices, ablution facilities and the sick bay are located on the ground floor for easy, convenient access. The Grade R – Grade 3 classrooms are arranged to allow for the wide variety of activities which can take place. The School has excellent facilities, we have eight well equipped classrooms, a sick room, three administration offices and one staff room. This allows the doors to be locked shut during the school day and additionally allow us to monitor the whole site from the school office. The site has sound-proofed outdoor classrooms to ensure minimal disturbances from the ever busy adjoining streets. The school is secured with a top of the range security system and we have installed CCTV cameras on the entrance doors near and inside the classrooms and various other locations around the school site. The School playground area is only two streets away from the school, and is marked out with games and landscaped on soft surfaces for the children to play on. The playground is secure and safe for the extra-curricular activities.

Awesome Teachings

Stenham Private School aims to provide the highest and unequalled top class training available in today’s competitive global village through forming highly intelligible strategic alliances with captains of all departments of education and training who share the same vision and core values as us. Stenham Private School distinguishes itself by providing focused, top quality and cost effective education services to shape global minds in a South African Soul. The School aims to maintain a high quality of teaching, good pupil achievement rates, impeccable pupil behavior, pupils that are courteous and polite through ensuring that the school leaders, governors and staff work together with determination to achieve the above. Stenham Private School celebrates and values different pupil cultural backgrounds, different religions and different languages through encouraging the children to be proud of their own culture, religion and language and through showing respect to the diversity of others.

Educational

Stenham Private School currently has one location in the West of Pretoria, from where administration and learning is rendered. The School offers education services for Grade 1 – Grade 7 levels. Classroom interventions are presented on a group presentation, and on a one on one teacher to pupil basis. The School is open to the entire nation of South Africa and the School's major clients include pupils from the surrounding suburbs, parents of pupils working close and around the school and learners around the province as a whole.

Educational

Our vision is to expand the School to offer all grade levels from Grade 1 to Grade 12 (Stenham Private Primary and High School). We intend to produce pupils of a high-quality standard that meet the requirements of the Gauteng Department of Education, pupils that demonstrate integrity and respect. We are also aiming to expand nationally, opening Stenham Private Primary School branches in all provinces, for all pupils in South Africa to have access to our Education Services.
